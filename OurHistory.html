Our Journey

Manscaped was established in 2016 to provide male grooming products targeted at pubic hair removal.[1] The company uses the term manscaping, a long-standing neologism, as the basis of its branding, and is referenced in the Merriam-Webster definition of the word.[2] It has also capitalized on the shock factor of addressing male hygiene taboos, alongside the use of pun-based humour and paid celebrity appearances, to build its name.[3]
